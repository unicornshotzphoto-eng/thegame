## Troubleshooting

### "No friends yet" message appears
**Solution:**
1. Go to Friends section (ğŸ‘¥)
2. Add or accept friend requests
3. Return to Gardens
4. Friend list will now show

### Friend doesn't appear in list
**Possible causes:**
- They haven't accepted your friend request yet
- You haven't added them yet
- They may have removed you

**Solution:**
1. Check Friends section to confirm they're listed
2. Refresh Gardens (pull down and release)
3. Close and reopen the app

### Can't see the friend picker
**Solution:**
1. Ensure Friends API is loading correctly
2. Check internet connection
3. Try refreshing the form
4. Close and reopen the app
